NARUTO

Summary

In the Hidden Leaf Village, a mischievous orphan named Naruto Uzumaki dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of the village. However, he is shunned by the villagers because he carries the Nine-Tailed Fox, a powerful beast sealed inside him at birth. Determined to prove himself, Naruto joins Team 7 alongside the genius Sasuke Uchiha and the intelligent Sakura Haruno, under the leadership of the legendary ninja Kakashi Hatake. As they embark on dangerous missions, Naruto faces powerful enemies, uncovers hidden secrets about his past, and develops unbreakable bonds with his friends.
